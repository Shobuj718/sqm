# SQM Company Knowledge Base

## Company Overview

SQM is an online retail brand that sells lifestyle, fashion, beauty, home, and gadget products through Facebook pages, Messenger, comments, and online order channels.

The company focuses on fast response, clear product information, reliable delivery, and helpful after-sales support. Customer support agents should reply politely, keep messages concise, and avoid making promises that are not confirmed by company policy or stock status.

If a customer asks about a product, first identify the product name, size, color, variant, and quantity. If the customer wants to order, collect name, phone number, full delivery address, product variant, and payment preference.

## Company Contact Information

Support hours are 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM, Saturday through Thursday.

Support is closed on Friday except for urgent delivery or payment issues.

Customers can contact support by Facebook inbox, page comment, or phone support if a phone number is provided by the page.

If a customer asks for office address, reply that order and support are handled online unless an official pickup location is confirmed for that page.

## Reply Style Rules

Use friendly, short, professional replies.

For public Facebook comments, keep replies very short and invite the customer to inbox if personal information is needed.

Never ask for phone number, full address, transaction ID, or private order details in a public comment. Ask the customer to send those details in inbox.

Do not confirm stock unless product availability is known.

Do not confirm refund, replacement, warranty, free delivery, or discount unless the policy clearly allows it.

If the customer is angry, apologize first, then ask for the order ID or details in inbox.

## Delivery Policy

Inside Dhaka delivery normally takes 1 to 2 business days after confirmation.

Outside Dhaka delivery normally takes 2 to 4 business days after confirmation.

Delivery may be delayed because of courier backlog, holidays, weather, incomplete address, unreachable phone number, or stock transfer delay.

Inside Dhaka delivery charge is 80 BDT.

Outside Dhaka delivery charge is 130 BDT.

Free delivery applies only when a product page, campaign, or agent confirms free delivery for that specific offer.

## Payment Methods

Available payment methods:

1. Cash on delivery
2. bKash
3. Nagad
4. Card payment, if checkout supports it

For advance payment orders, ask the customer to share the transaction ID in inbox after payment.

If a customer says payment failed but money was deducted, ask for transaction ID, sender number, payment time, and order ID.

## Return Policy

Return requests are accepted within 3 days of delivery.

The product must be unused, undamaged, clean, and returned with original packaging, tags, accessories, and invoice if available.

Used products, damaged-by-customer products, washed clothing, opened beauty products, hygiene-sensitive products, and products without packaging are not eligible for return.

If the company sent the wrong product or a defective product, the issue will be reviewed after the customer shares photo or video proof.

## Exchange Policy

Exchange requests are accepted within 3 days of delivery.

Exchange depends on stock availability.

For size, color, or preference exchange, delivery charge may apply.

If the wrong product was sent by the company, the company may arrange replacement after verification.

## Refund Policy

Refunds are processed after the returned product is received and inspected.

Approved refunds usually take 7 to 10 business days.

Delivery charge is non-refundable unless the company sent the wrong or defective product.

## Order Confirmation Script

Before confirming an order, collect all required information and repeat the order summary to the customer.

Example confirmation:

Your order is for UrbanFit Cotton T-Shirt, black, XL, quantity 1. Delivery address is inside Dhaka, delivery charge 80 BDT. Please confirm your phone number and full address to proceed.

## Complaint Handling Script

If the customer reports wrong product, damaged product, missing item, late delivery, or payment issue, apologize and ask for the required details in inbox.

Do not argue publicly.

Example damaged product reply:

We are sorry for the inconvenience. Please inbox us with your order ID, phone number, and clear photos or video so our team can review it.

Example late delivery reply:

Sorry for the delay. Please share your order ID or registered phone number in inbox, and we will check the courier update.

## Data Privacy Rule

Never ask customers to share phone number, full address, transaction ID, or payment screenshot in public comments.

For public comments, reply with "Please inbox us" when private details are needed.

## Agent Escalation Rules

Escalate to a manager if:

1. Customer threatens legal action
2. Customer reports repeated failed delivery
3. Customer claims payment was deducted but order was not created
4. Customer asks for bulk order discount
5. Customer reports product injury, safety issue, or serious defect
6. Customer is a public influencer, media person, or page admin of another business

## Bulk Order Rules

For bulk orders, ask for product name, quantity, delivery location, and deadline.

Do not confirm bulk discount without manager approval.

Suggested reply:

Thank you for your interest in bulk order. Please inbox us with product name, quantity, and delivery location so our team can check the best offer.
