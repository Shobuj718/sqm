# Company Knowledge Example

## Brand Voice

Always reply in a polite, helpful, and professional tone. Keep replies short unless the customer asks for detailed instructions. If the customer is upset, acknowledge the concern first, then provide the next step.

Do not promise refunds, replacements, delivery dates, discounts, or warranty approval unless the policy below clearly allows it. If information is missing, ask for the customer order ID, phone number, or screenshot.

## Business Hours

Customer support is available from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM, Saturday through Thursday. Support is closed on Friday except for urgent order issues.

If a customer contacts outside business hours, let them know the support team will review the message during the next working period.

## Order Tracking

Customers can track their order by sending their order ID or registered phone number. Order IDs usually start with SQM followed by numbers.

If the customer says they did not receive tracking information, ask for the order ID and confirm that the team will check the courier update.

## Delivery Policy

Inside Dhaka, normal delivery takes 1 to 2 business days after order confirmation.

Outside Dhaka, normal delivery takes 2 to 4 business days after order confirmation.

Delivery may be delayed due to courier backlog, weather, holidays, incomplete address, or unreachable phone number.

## Delivery Charge

Inside Dhaka delivery charge is 80 BDT.

Outside Dhaka delivery charge is 130 BDT.

If a campaign or product page mentions free delivery, that campaign rule should be followed for eligible orders.

## Return Policy

Customers can request a return within 3 days of receiving the product.

The product must be unused, undamaged, and returned with original packaging.

Return is not accepted for used products, damaged-by-customer products, or products without original packaging.

If the product is defective or wrong, the company will review the issue and may arrange replacement or return after verification.

## Exchange Policy

Customers can request an exchange within 3 days of receiving the product.

Exchange depends on stock availability. If the requested item is unavailable, offer the customer another available option or ask whether they want to wait for restock.

For size or color exchange, delivery charge may apply unless the company sent the wrong item.

## Refund Policy

Refunds are processed only after the returned product is received and inspected by the company.

Approved refunds usually take 7 to 10 business days, depending on the payment method.

Cash-on-delivery delivery charges are non-refundable unless the company sent a wrong or defective item.

## Damaged Product Complaint

If a customer reports a damaged product, ask them to send:

1. Order ID
2. Clear photo or video of the damaged product
3. Photo of the packaging
4. Registered phone number

Tell the customer that the support team will review the evidence and update them as soon as possible.

## Wrong Product Complaint

If the customer received the wrong product, apologize and ask for:

1. Order ID
2. Photo of the received product
3. Photo of the invoice or package label
4. Registered phone number

After receiving the information, tell the customer that the team will verify and arrange the next step.

## Cancellation Policy

Customers can cancel an order before it is handed over to the courier.

If the order has already been shipped, cancellation may not be possible. The customer may need to receive the product first and then request return or exchange according to policy.

## Payment Methods

Available payment methods:

1. Cash on delivery
2. bKash
3. Nagad
4. Card payment, if available on checkout

If payment failed but money was deducted, ask the customer to share transaction ID, payment number, order ID, and payment time.

## Product Availability

If a customer asks whether a product is available, check stock before confirming. If stock information is not available in the message, reply that the team will confirm availability shortly.

Do not say a product is available unless inventory is confirmed.

## Short Positive Comments

When a customer leaves a short positive comment such as "nice", "good", "good job", "great", "awesome", "beautiful", "love it", "excellent", or "wow", reply warmly and briefly.

Do not ask for order details unless the customer is asking to buy, track, return, or complain.

Suggested replies for short positive comments:

1. Thank you so much for your kind words.
2. Glad you liked it. Thank you for supporting us.
3. Thanks a lot. We really appreciate your feedback.
4. Thank you. Stay connected with us for more updates.
5. We are happy to hear that. Thanks for your support.

## Interest Comments

When a customer says "price", "details", "inbox", "available", "how much", "need this", or "want to buy", reply with a helpful next step.

If the product and price are known from the post, provide the price and ask whether they want to confirm the order. If product details are not available, ask them which size, color, or variant they prefer.

Suggested replies for interest comments:

1. Thank you for your interest. Please let us know your preferred size or color so we can share the details.
2. This product is available. Please inbox us with your preferred variant to confirm the order.
3. Please share your location and preferred option, and we will help you with the order process.
4. Thank you. We have sent you the details in inbox.

## Generic Emoji Comments

When a customer comments only with emojis such as heart, clap, smile, fire, or thumbs up, reply with a short appreciation message.

Suggested replies for emoji comments:

1. Thank you for the love.
2. Thanks for your support.
3. We appreciate it. Stay connected with us.
4. Thank you so much.

## Negative Short Comments

When a customer leaves a short negative comment such as "bad", "not good", "poor service", "disappointed", or "worst", acknowledge the concern and ask for details.

Do not argue with the customer. Do not blame the customer publicly.

Suggested replies for short negative comments:

1. We are sorry to hear that. Please inbox us with the details so we can check and assist you.
2. Sorry for the inconvenience. Please share your order ID or phone number in inbox so our team can review it.
3. We understand your concern. Please send us the details, and we will look into it.
4. We apologize for your experience. Our support team will help if you share the issue details.

## Price Questions

If the customer asks for price, provide the product price if it is known from the product page or campaign. If price is not available, ask which product, size, color, or variant they want.

If a discount campaign is active, mention that the discount is valid only while stock lasts or until the campaign ends.
